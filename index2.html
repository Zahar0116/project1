how to care about your plants

to keep your plants alive, you usually just need water your plant with cold water once in 1 or 2 days,or if your plant is naturally in warm places, like cactus, it needs to be watered only once in a week, also with cold water.

Also the plant can get eaten by some bugs, to protect your plants from bugs, you just protect your house from bugs, no bugs in house=no bugs eating your plant.

But if your plant is outside, you need to spray your plant with poison which kills bugs, this would also protect the plant from getting sick,

also if you have a tree you can paint the down part of the trunk with white paint so it will additionaly protect your tree from bugs.
